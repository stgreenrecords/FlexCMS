Legacy of Excellence

Our Origin.

Founded on the principles of ballistic-grade engineering and the pursuit of silent velocity, TUT emerged from a private atelier in 1994.

The Precision Atelier

Every chassis is machined from a single block of aerospace-grade aluminum. We do not manufacture; we curate mechanical symphonies.

The Philosophy

"Luxury is not the presence of everything, but the absence of the unnecessary. We build for those who find power in silence."

At TUT, we believe that the soul of a vehicle is found in its smallest tolerances. While others focus on mass production, our atelier limits output to ensure each vehicle receives four thousand hours of hand-finishing.

Our engineers are artisans. Our designers are architects. Together, they create machines that transcend utility to become kinetic sculptures, designed to withstand the test of time and trend.

1994

The Monolith Chassis

Introduction of the first unified carbon-fiber cell for consumer vehicles, setting a new benchmark for structural rigidity and occupant safety.

2012

Acoustic Isolation Suite

The debut of our patented active-nullification glass, achieving a cabin noise floor of less than 15 decibels at cruising speed.

2024

The Flux Drive

A solid-state battery architecture providing 800 miles of range with zero-impact thermal management.
