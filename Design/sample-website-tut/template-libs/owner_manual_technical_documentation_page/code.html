Chapter 01

Integrated Safety Systems

The structural integrity of the chassis is augmented by an array of intelligent sensors designed to anticipate and mitigate impact forces before they occur.

Our proprietary Safety Matrix utilizes three-dimensional spatial awareness to monitor surroundings 2,000 times per second. This data is fed directly into the kinematic control module, adjusting suspension damping and braking readiness in real-time.
