Can I terminate my lease early?

Our Performance and Pure tiers include the 'Agile Exit' clause, allowing for early termination or vehicle swap after 12 months with minimal adjustment fees, provided equity thresholds are met.

Is there a mileage limitation?

Standard programs offer 7,500, 10,000, and 12,000 mile annual limits. However, we offer bespoke high-mileage contracts tailored for transcontinental drivers.

How does tax treatment work for Electric models?

All federal and state incentives are factored into the gross capitalized cost reduction, ensuring that the tax benefits are immediately realized in your monthly payment structure.
