Frequently Asked Questions

Service intervals for Electric models?

Unlike internal combustion counterparts, TUT EVs require full inspection every 24 months, with telemetry monitoring handling minor adjustments in between.

Warranty coverage on components?

All precision components are covered under a 6-year inclusive excellence guarantee, including brake systems and adaptive suspension elements.

Is concierge service available globally?

Concierge retrieval is available within a 150-mile radius of any authorized TUT Atelier. Specialized transport can be arranged beyond this zone.

Software version rollback?

In the event of a specific preference for legacy driving dynamics, our technicians can manually re-flash previous firmware iterations during an on-site visit.
